Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you submit a form or sign up for our newsletter. This information may include your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, such as your IP address, the type of browser and operating system you are using, and the pages you visit on our site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide you with a better user experience and to communicate with you. For example, we may use your email address to send you updates and newsletters about our website.

We may also use your information to analyze how our website is being used and to improve our services.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as website hosting and email delivery.

We may also share your information if required by law or as necessary to protect our legal rights.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your user experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our site. We use cookies to remember your preferences and to personalize your experience on our website.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
